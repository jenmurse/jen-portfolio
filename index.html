Jen Murse

Jen is a multidisciplinary designer and creative leader whose work spans digital and physical mediums with a focus on brand, narrative, and technology. Over the past 20 years, she has evolved from a hands-on designer into a leader of high-performing teams, creating everything from finely crafted objects to large-scale brand experiences. Most recently, she led brand creative and production at Block. She continues to evolve her practice through the intersection of craft and emerging technology.

When she is not pushing pixels, she enjoys a good cup of coffee, traveling the world, and tinkering on a range of projects with her husband, Garth.
